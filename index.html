This isn't just a store.
It's a feeling.

Walking into Memory Lane feels a little like stepping into a warm memory — the kind that makes you slow down, breathe a little deeper, and enjoy the simple pleasure of browsing. It's a comfortable, welcoming space where you can wander through more than 60 curated booths, reminisce a little, and hunt for treasures you didn't even know you were looking for.

What makes our shop different is the care behind it. We're not the dusty, forgotten kind of antique mall. Our vendors keep their spaces fresh, rotate in new finds, and let old inventory move along so the store always feels vibrant, alive, and easy to shop. People tell us all the time how clean the store is, how simple it is to navigate, and how they always find the coolest stuff — and that means the world to us.

And of course, there are the dogs. Itty Bitty and Pneuma greet new friends every day and have become a beloved part of the Memory Lane experience, adding their own charm to the music, the atmosphere, and the feeling that this is a place made with care.

Whether you're here for serious antiques or whimsical weekend finds, we hope you feel what so many others do — that Memory Lane is a family-run dream brought to life, a place where the treasures change often, the welcome is genuine, and every visit feels a little special.

Furniture & Décor

Mid-century modern, Victorian, farmhouse, and everything in between. If it has character, it has a home here.

The Monthly
Outdoor Market

Once a month, we open our space to the public for a full-on outdoor market. Dozens of vendors, morning light, maybe a little live music, and the particular joy of walking through rows of tables wondering what you're about to find.

Whether you're a seasoned picker or just curious, the market is one of the best Saturday mornings Stillwater has to offer.
